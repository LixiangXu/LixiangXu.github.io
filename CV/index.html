Profile

I'm interested in applying computational methods such as machine learning and statistical modeling on human brain data to explore human brain functions.
I just received Ph.D @ physics from the University of Texas at Austin in 2022. Now I'm a postdoc in Prof. Dean Mobbs' lab @ Caltech, studying human behavior and brain functions related to emotion, anxiety, fear and decision making under threat with behavioral experiments, fMRI data and computational models.
In my graduate school, before 2017 I studied quantum physics and atomic thin layer materials, and after 2017 I worked human brain functions with brain data and machine learning methods, especially about how human brain processes natural language.
